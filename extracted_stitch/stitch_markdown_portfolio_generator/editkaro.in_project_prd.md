# Project Requirements Document (PRD): Editkaro.in Creative Agency Site

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** Editkaro.in Website Redesign & Expansion  
**Brand Identity:** Premium, high-octane social media marketing and video editing agency.  
**Target Audience:** Content creators, YouTubers, eCommerce brands, and corporate marketing teams seeking high-retention video content.  
**Objective:** To build a high-performance, multi-page web experience that showcases editing expertise, simplifies the onboarding process, and provides a transparent pricing/client management portal.

---

## 2. Visual & Brand Identity
*   **Design Theme:** "Dark Nebula Cyber"
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Base:** Deep Dark Violet/Black (`#030307` to `#0A0A14`).
    *   **Accents (Neon):** Cyber Purple (`#A855F7`), Cyber Pink (`#EC4899`), Electric Cyan (`#06B6D4`).
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headers:** Outfit (Futuristic, Bold).
    *   **Body:** Inter/Syne (Clean, Modern).
*   **Aesthetic Principles:** Glassmorphism (backdrop-blur), neon glowing borders, asymmetrical card layouts, and cinematic motion effects.

## 4. Technical Requirements
*   **Interactivity:** Vanilla JavaScript for sliders, video review notes, and mouse-tracking "cyber glow" effects.
*   **Responsiveness:** Desktop-first design with planned mobile adaptations for vertical video viewing.
*   **Performance:** Lightweight IntersectionObserver for scroll animations to maintain high SEO scores.

---

## 5. Success Metrics
*   User conversion from Home ➡️ Pricing Calculator.
*   Reduced friction in lead intake via the multi-step booking portal.
*   Clear communication of "High-Retention" value proposition through visual sliders.
